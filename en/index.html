Frequently Asked Questions

What age can children start drinking?

Suitable for children aged 1 and above
who have already started eating a variety of foods.

Can it be drunk daily? Any side effects?

We recommend 1–2 boxes per day
to supplement vitamins and nutrients from fruits and vegetables.

Can it replace milk?

We don't recommend using it as a milk replacement, as juice
doesn't contain as much calcium and protein as milk. But it can be
enjoyed as a daily supplement or snack
for a wider variety of nutrients.

Will children develop a sweet tooth?

NOMINOMI has no added sugar
and is 45% less sweet than regular formulas.
Choosing a less-sweet drink from early childhood
helps reduce the risk of sugar dependency in the future.

Can children with allergies drink it?

Please read the ingredients first. If your child is allergic to certain fruits
such as apple or lemon, please avoid.
(Our formula contains no lactose, nuts, or gluten.)

Does it need refrigeration? How long does it keep?

No refrigeration needed before opening. After opening, refrigerate
and consume within 24 hours for freshness
and safety.
